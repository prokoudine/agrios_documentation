Purchase
========

This page provides a walkthrough for users on how to manage purchases in AgriOS/Odoo.

Contents
--------

.. contents::
    :local:
    :depth: 2

Menu Structure
--------------

- Purchase
    - Requests for Quotation
    - Purchase Orders
    - Vendors
    - Products
    - Invoices
    - Reports

Walkthrough
-----------

1. **Accessing Purchase Module**
   - Go to the "Purchase" menu from the dashboard.

2. **Creating a Request for Quotation**
   - Click on "Requests for Quotation" and select "Create".
   - Fill in vendor and product details.

3. **Managing Purchase Orders**
   - Confirm RFQs to create purchase orders.
   - Track order status and delivery.

4. **Vendor Management**
   - Add or edit vendor information.

5. **Product Management**
   - Manage products and pricing for purchases.

6. **Invoicing**
   - Generate and track purchase invoices.

7. **Reporting**
   - View purchase analytics and reports.
